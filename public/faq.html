Digiful - FAQ

  • What does digiful do for my shop?

    Digijul solves the problem of fulfillment, storage, communication, and shop integration of your digital products.

    • Communication & fulfillment: Customers receive an email upon checkout with a secure link to the download page.
    • Storage: Files are securely hosted on Amazon's AWS S3 storage.
    • Shop integration: A simple form allows your to add a new product to your shop, make it active, and upload the file for hosting, all in one step.

  • How can I change subscriptions?

    Unsubscribe from the Settings page in the admin app. Then select a different subscription.

  • How does the self hosting plan work?

    Self hosting requires you to setup your own bucket in AWS S3, and generate limited-scope credentials for that bucket. Next you'll fill out S3 credentials form on the Settings page of the app. Your secret key will be encrypted while stored.

  • Is there any way to know which files in my shop were created by digiful?

    Yes. Every product created by dijiful gets the tag 'digiful' applied to it.
